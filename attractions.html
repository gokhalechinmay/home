Being a hub for many industries and cultures, Toronto naturally has many famous attractions for which travellers from around the would visit. Here are a few examples that are worth visiting:

  • CN Tower

    This tourist spot used to be the highest free-standing structure in the world, at a height of about 500m. It is one of the most popular visiting sites in the city, tourists go up the building in an elevator, and test their bravery by standing on a glass floor.

    Purchase tickets here.

  • Royal Ontario Museum (ROM)

    This interesting site is one of the largest museums in North America. Various exhibitions such as ones about Ancient Egypt, and the animal kingdom are held here. Definitely worth a visit!

    Purchase tickets here.

  • Casa Loma

    This historic site has an architecture unlike any other. Its unique structure represents the history of Toronto as a pioneer location for European settlers and a place for industrial and financial development. A great place to get some pictures!

    Purchase tickets here.
