  1. General

    • The city of Toronto is located in the province of Ontario, in the country of Canada. Being the largest city in the country, Toronto is home to approximately 3 million, with an extended population of about 6 million in the GTA (Greater Toronto Area). The GTA consists of various suburbs of Toronto including Scarborough, Etobicoke, & North York.
    • Toronto is the true embodiment of the multicultural nature of Canada, as it is home to people of various nationalities who live in a state of harmony & acceptance.
    • Toronto is a centre for various industries including banking, technology, film, arts & culture. As a result, it has become a hub for both immigrants & tourism.

  2. Geography

    Toronto is located on the North shore of Lake Ontario, & extends northwards from there. It spans an area of 630km2, with the neighbouring cities of Brampton, Mississauga, Pickering, Markham, & Vaughan.

  3. Climate

    Toronto experience extremely hot and humid summers, and incredibly cold winters. The average summer temperature is 25℃, and the average winter temperature is −15℃. Winters are generally accompanied by considerable snowfall, with occasional storms of hail and rain. Summers are quite humid, and can cause discomfort for the average Toronto resident.
    Lake Ontario helps keep the Toronto climate in check. Without the lake, the climate conditions may be even more severe.
